# Control Mappings for TeleOp

Gamepad 1 – Init (before start)

|Control|When|Action|
|---|---|---|
|Left bumper|Tap|Set BLUE alliance|
|Right bumper|Tap|Set RED alliance|

>**Note**: Gamepad1 will rumble at the start of auto AND teleop to remind the driver to select an alliance.

Gamepad 1 – TeleOp (after start)

|Control|When|Action|
|---|---|---|
|Left stick (x,y)|Hold|Drive: forward/back + strafe left/right|
|Right stick (x)|Hold|Turn robot left/right|
|Right bumper|Hold|Slow mode: drive at 40% speed|
|D-pad Up|Hold|Auto aim: Hold to stay in place and turn to scoring goal. Release to drive normally|

Gamepad 2 – Init (before start)

|Control|When|Action|
|---|---|---|
|–|–|–|

Gamepad 2 – TeleOp (after start)

|Control|When|Action|
|---|---|---|
|Triangle|Tap|Set flywheel to FAR power preset|
|Cross|Tap|Set flywheel to CLOSE power preset|
|D-pad Up|Tap|Spin flywheel to current preset|
|D-pad Down|Tap|Stop flywheel|
|Right trigger|Hold (variable)|Intake IN (more trigger = faster)|
|Right bumper|Hold|Intake OUT slowly (clear balls)|
|Square|Hold|Shoot: run Transfer + Intake IN to feed flywheel. Release to stop|
